About biocycle

Our platform was created by a team of passionate waste management experts and sustainability advocates committed to making composting accessible and enjoyable for everyone. Whether you're a seasoned composter or just getting started, our tools and resources will help you compost confidently and achieve your composting goals. At Biocycle, our services include: A composting calculator that helps you calculate the ideal proportions of organic materials for your compost pile based on your specific needs and goals. Educational materials, including articles, videos, and workshops, help you learn the basics of composting and stay up-to-date on the latest trends and techniques. Analytics and reporting tools that track your composting progress and help you measure your environmental impact. Customization options allow you to tailor your composting experience to your unique needs, preferences, and location.
