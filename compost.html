ABOUT BIOCYCLE

Why choose BIOCYCLE

Sustainablilty

We operate on the principles of sustainability, collaboration, and innovation. By working together and harnessing the power of technology, we can create an equitable, efficient, and effective waste management system. Our team comprises waste management experts, sustainability advocates, and composting enthusiasts dedicated to making a positive impact on the world

Smart cities

At BioCycle, we believe that waste management can be a catalyst for positive change. We are passionate about creating a world where waste is minimized, resources are conserved, and communities are empowered to take action. Our mission is to provide innovative, sustainable, and effective waste management solutions that benefit people and the planet.

Waste management through Ideal Composting

Bio-Cycle provides innovative and sustainable waste management solutions, focusing on composting, on empowering communities and businesses to reduce waste and conserve resources.
Our vision is a world where waste is viewed not as a problem but an opportunity. An opportunity to create jobs, reduce pollution, and regenerate the natural systems that sustain us all. Composting is a key component of this vision, providing a simple, scalable, and effective way to turn waste into a valuable resource.

Waste Type Selection

Allow users to select the type of waste they want to compost, such as food waste, fooder and sludge, agricultural waste and by-products, Livestock waste.

Input fields for compost pile size

Allow users to input the size of their compost pile, such as the length, width, and heigh

Composting time

Allow users to input the amount of time they want to spend composting, such as 1 month, 3 months, or 6 months.

Calculation of ideal proportions

Based on the user's inputs, calculate the ideal proportions of organic materials for composting, such as a 3:1 ratio of brown to green materials
